About Me!

I'm Hailey, a Tuolumne native and lover of our forest.

Hi! I'm a Web Developer focused on helping local businesses thrive. I have a degree in writing and have studied marketing and brand development as well, leading up to my eventual studies in coding to bring it all together. I am currently offering Full Website Development as well as counseling in SEO, Domain Services, and general website help. I've audited and improved websites, leading to an increase in views and traffic. Having a dual background in creative writing and coding means that I work from a unique perspective. I believe my appreciation for the art of words helps me come up with creative solutions to virtual problems. After all, coding itself is a kind of poetry. So I am always taking care to find the most practical and elegant solution to whatever I'm faced with.

I grew up in Sonora, was born and raised here. So I'm not only familiar with local businesses but I frequent them, and know some of their owners. It's an intimate relationship one has with their hometown, and it's my hope that I can be a part of this town's growth. Everything is online now whether we like it or not, and having a well-made website can make a huge difference in terms of visibility and traffic.

As for who I am? I'm an avid reader, gamer, and maker. If something can be done myself, you can bet I'll be doing it. I love the outdoors and the forest, like everyone else in this town :) I snowboard when I can and swim when I can't, and at the end of the day I'm curled up with some fantasy book or game.
